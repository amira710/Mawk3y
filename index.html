"HEALNET APPLICATION"

HEALNET is a telemedicine application designed to make healthcare more accessible and organized.
It allows patients to connect with doctors online for chronic diseases that can be treated from home, or book offline visits for in-clinic treatments.
The app also provides a complete medical record for each patient, so all visits, prescriptions, and test results are stored in one place.
In addition, HealNet supports healthcare authorities with tools to monitor new diseases and public health trends.
